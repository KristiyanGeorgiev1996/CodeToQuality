There are different methodologies for software development. **Heavyweight (Plan-Driven) methods** like Waterfall or V-Model rely on detailed documentation and formal processes. They work well when requirements are stable but are less flexible to change.  

**Agile methodologies**, on the other hand, are lightweight and iterative. They break development into small cycles (iterations), where small portions of software are developed, tested, and delivered quickly. Agile emphasizes **customer collaboration, frequent increments, and adaptability to change**.
